The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus, the interim Chief Adviser of Bangladesh, has emphasized the collective responsibility of building a new Bangladesh, inspired by the sacrifices of individuals like Abu Sayeed. He stated, "This is our responsibility to build the new Bangladesh," urging all citizens to unite in this endeavor. Dr. Yunus envisions a Bangladesh where everyone has equal opportunities to thrive, emphasizing the need for inclusivity and innovation. He believes that empowering the youth and fostering entrepreneurship are key to building a prosperous and equitable society. His call for unity and sustainability highlights the importance of collective action in addressing challenges and creating a brighter future for the nation.
